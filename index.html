Tribute Page

This tribute page celebrates one of my favorite series, Rick and Morty. It captures the show's unique blend of dark humor, science fiction, and philosophical depth. For this project, I utilized ChatGPT to generate the content and designed a webpage with a navigation bar featuring functional buttons. The design is straightforward, incorporating a photo, minimal typography, and well-organized sections of information.

Technical Documentation Page

This technical documentation page provides essential information on basic HTML and CSS. I used ChatGPT to generate the content and designed the webpage along with its styling. The page includes a sidebar with functional buttons, allowing users to easily navigate to different sections.

Product Landing Page

This product landing page showcases the fictional brand “Scooty,” which I contributed on creating as part of a college course. I used ChatGPT to generate the text and designed the webpage, featuring a functional navigation bar fixed at the top of the viewport. The page includes the brand title and tagline with a video background, sections of formatted information, and an email input form for subscriptions.
